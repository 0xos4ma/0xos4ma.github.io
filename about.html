About Me

Aspiring Red Team & Cybersecurity Learner with a passion for ethical hacking and security research

Aspiring Red Team & Cybersecurity Learner

Hi, I’m Osama Abd Al Naby, a 16-year-old aspiring learner in Red Team and cybersecurity.

I focus on ethical hacking, vulnerability research, and hands-on labs to build real-world skills.

I publish proof-of-concepts, write clear explainers, and develop small tools to help others understand security.

When I’m not (ethically) breaking into systems, I enjoy documenting my journey through technical writing, blogging, and contributing to the cybersecurity community. My long-term goal is to grow into a Red Team specialist while giving back through open-source projects and collaborative research

I don’t yet have formal industry experience, but I’m building my foundation through projects, labs, and persistence — and I’m always open to mentorship, collaboration, and opportunities to learn.
